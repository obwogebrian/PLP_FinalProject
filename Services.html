Services Offered

Chess Trainig

I am a qualified chess trainer with the Developmental Instructor title and a license valid till December 2024 from the International Chess Federation (FIDE). I can teach beginners aged 6+ how to play chess.

Both individual and group classes for children and adults will be offered very soon within the neighbourhoods of Ujamaa, Likoni, Kitui, kwavonza, Km, and Nzeu, within Kitui County. 

Online options will follow after in-person training has been running for a few months at least.

Chess Event Organisation

I am an experienced chess organiser who has organised 35 events since January 2012. I can organise chess events such as tournaments (unrated and FIDE-rated), simultaneous exhibition matches, and more in my own capacity as an individual, on behalf of organisations, or in partnership with organisations.

Chess Arbiting

I am a qualified chess arbiter with the International Arbiter title and a license valid for life from the International Chess Federation (FIDE). I can officiate in chess tournaments (unrated and FIDE-rated) as an arbiter.
