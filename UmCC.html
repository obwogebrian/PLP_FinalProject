Umoja Chess Club (UmCC)

Umoja Chess Club (abbreviated as ‘UmCC’) was founded in mid-2011 by yours truly (Obwoge Brian) as a group of chess enthusiasts based in/around Likoni who met on Wednesday evenings at Nyali Mall to play and discuss games. 

In 2012, the club moved to a different location—Kitui Coffee World—where members met on Saturday afternoons to play friendly games and for training sessions. Several tournaments were also organised at this venue. 

The club maintained activity till late 2014/early 2015 even with changes in leadership but it couldn’t be sustained and the club became defunct.
