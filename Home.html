Chess Trainer, Organiser, and Mc in Likoni, Mombasa

Hello there and welcome to my website!

My name is Obwoge Brian and I am a freelance chess professional (coach, organiser, and referee) based in Ujamaa, Likoni, Kenya.

Since 2010, I have taught beginners to play chess, organised chess events, refereed in chess tournaments, reported on chess as a photographer, and columnist, founded chess clubs, sold chess equipment, and more.

After spending two years in Mombasa, eleven months in likoni, and nine months in Ujamaa between November 2018 and Aguast 2022, I moved back to Kitui on 20th September 2022 and am now based here permanently.

To know more about me and my interest in chess, feel free to browse through the following sections/pages: About Umoja, Services Offered, Events, Blog + News, UmCC, Resources, and Contact.
